# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We, as contributors and maintainers of this project, pledge to make participation in our community a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, appearance, race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, or level of experience.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and considerate in discussions
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Focusing on what is best for the project and the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or offensive comments
- Personal attacks or insulting language
- Publishing othersâ€™ private information without permission
- Any behavior that could be considered unprofessional or inappropriate

## Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behavior and are expected to take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including GitHub repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other communication channels related to this project.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainer. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
